Hello and welcome to my first-ever lasagne recipe, which is so delicious and easy to make for you and for your family/guests to enjoy on a lovely sunny afternoon. So, let's get started!

Ingredients for the Bolognese Sauce

  • 500g Bolognese sauce/ but you can use tomato paste if you want
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 400g mince meat
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 2 teaspoons mixed herbs
  • 2 teaspoon salt/ salt to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper- for flavour
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes/ optional
  • 1 tablespoon garlic and ginger paste/ adjust to likings
  • 2 tablespoon tomato puree
  • 1 carrot ( grate the carrot to remove meaty smell)
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • A medium sized tray or pot

    • White sauce for the lasagne

    • 1 tablespoon butter
    • 1 tablespoon plain flour
    • 2 cups milk
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt/salt to taste
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic granules
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion granules
    • 1 cup cheddar cheese
    • 20 lasagne sheets
    • 700g mozzarella cheese
    • Parsely for garnish

    Instructions

    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cook the lasagne sheets according to package instructions (usually for about 5 mins, keep stirring to avoid them sticking together). Drain and set aside.
    • In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground beef, onion, ginger, and garlic paste. Cook until beef is browned and onion is translucent.
    • Add salt, chili powder, pepper, mixed herbs, carrot, Bolognese sauce, tomato puree, and water. Mix well and cook for about 30 minutes on medium heat.
    • While the mixture is cooking, prepare the white sauce: melt butter, add flour, stir carefully, then gradually add milk, salt, pepper, onion, and garlic granules. Finally, add cheese and cook for about 5 minutes.
    • Layer lasagne sheets on the tray, followed by Bolognese sauce, white sauce, and cheese.
    • Repeat the layers and finish with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 20 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
    • Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

      • And there you go! A delicious Lasagne ready for you and your family, Enjoy!
